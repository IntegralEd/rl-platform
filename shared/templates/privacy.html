Overview

The Be More like B'More Health Assistant is a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) developed in support of Baltimore's successful B'More Healthy Babies initiative. This AI assistant demonstrates how other cities and place-based partnerships can leverage similar technology to replicate Baltimore's achievements in improving community health outcomes.

Project Scope

This MVP:

  • Provides a proof of concept for AI-assisted health information delivery
  • Demonstrates scalable knowledge sharing between health initiatives
  • Supports the replication of Baltimore's successful health programs
  • Facilitates learning from B'More Healthy Babies' proven approaches

Data Handling

The assistant:

  • Processes queries using public health information
  • Does not collect or store personal health information
  • Uses secure databases for public health resources
  • Maintains user privacy and confidentiality

Contact Information

For questions about this MVP or to learn more about implementing similar solutions in your community, please contact the project team through GitHub.
